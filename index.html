Stuff I’ve done

During the day I design digital products across startups and consumer platforms.

Most recently I was a Product Designer at Quizizz, where I led the design of features like Paper Mode, Quizizz AI, and Avatars used by millions of students and teachers around the world.

Before that I worked on core systems at GetMega, designing LMS, wallet, and KYC experiences for a real money gaming platform, and helped redesign the restaurant ordering ecosystem at Summer (formerly Toast).

Earlier in my career I worked with engineers at NVIDIA designing data heavy internal tools for GPU testing workflows.

Alongside full time roles, I work with early stage teams helping shape products from idea to launch.
